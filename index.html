About Me

I believe that this change in direction concentrating on the development of applications and software coding will be a natural progression and that my existing skills will be transferable once I am familiar with the new platforms and languages.

So far on the course I have enjoyed learning the Javascript and working in a team environment. The whole feel and atmosphere of this industry is welcoming and relaxed.
